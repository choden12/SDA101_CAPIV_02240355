My Education

In early 2011, I enrolled in class PP at Wombur Primary School, which is located in the lower part of Lhuntse Dzongkhag.

After that, I transferred to Autsho Middle Secondary School, where I stayed for almost two years, completing classes 1 and 2.

From there, I transferred to Minjey Middle Secondary School for my secondary education. I stayed there from classes 3 to 10.

Fortunately, I qualified for higher secondary school and transferred to Lhuntse Higher Secondary School, one of the most renowned in the country. I stayed there for two years.
